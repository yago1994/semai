Safari extension for Outlook Web

Remou helps clean up Outlook.com so email threads feel easier to scan and follow.

What this Extension does:

  1. Clean your email and display a chat view.
  2. Remove repeated signatures.

What it WON'T do

  1. Will NOT send messages on your behalf.
  2. Will NOT store or send your message data anywhere.

Steps to Activate

  1. Open Outlook in Safari.
  2. Click to open Safari Extensions.
  3. Enable Remou.
  4. You will see an icon appear on the left side of the search bar.
  5. Find the Extensions menu shown below.
  6. Click Always Allow for This Website.

What to expect

  • remou works on Outlook Web pages inside Safari.
  • When the extension is enabled, its tools appear in the Outlook interface.
  • If you do not see remou, refresh Outlook after turning the extension on.
