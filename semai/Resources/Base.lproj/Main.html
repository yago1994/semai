Safari extension for Outlook Web

This extension transforms your clutter-filled Outlook display into a modern chat-like interface so you can streamline your email communication.

You can turn on remou’s extension in Safari Extensions preferences.

remou’s extension is currently on. You can turn it off in Safari Extensions preferences.

remou’s extension is currently off. You can turn it on in Safari Extensions preferences.

Get started

  1. Open Safari settings and enable the remou extension.
  2. Open Outlook on the web in Safari, for example https://outlook.cloud.microsoft/mail/, and select an email thread.
  3. Turn your email conversations into easy chat-like communication.

What to expect

  • remou works on Outlook Web pages inside Safari.
  • When the extension is enabled, its tools appear in the Outlook interface.
  • If you do not see remou, refresh Outlook after turning the extension on.
  • This extension is in beta, and you may encounter errors. If you do, please share them.
